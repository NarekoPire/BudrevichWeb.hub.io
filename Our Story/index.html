Three generations of expertise, artisanship and creativity

What is Hatton Garden

Descended from a distinguished family of Russian jewellers, founder Arseniy Budrevich has been designing and crafting jewellery for almost 15 years. He learned his jewellery-making skills the traditional way, working alongside his father in his atelier with his brother, Eugene. With a keen interest in fine art that has influenced his aesthetic ever since, Arseniy was driven to continue the Budrevich family tradition and founded Budrevich Fine Jewellery Studio in London in 2015. A year later, Eugene joined Arseniy to enable the business to grow while keeping the entire jewellery-making process in-house. Using time-honoured skills complemented by the latest leading-edge technology, Budrevich quickly established itself as one of the leading jewellers in Hatton Garden — the historic heart of jewellery making in the capital. Experts in the fine art of jewellery craftsmanship, our customers include some of Bond Street’s biggest names, together with private clients across the world. Very few jewellery houses in London have all their craftsmen under one roof, as we do, which is why we often assist some of London’s most respected jewellery houses with diamond setting. In our design atelier, our team of craftsmen work side by side, allowing for a close dialogue between the artisans. Each has a speciality, with Eugene overseeing the metalwork and Arseniy the diamond setting.
