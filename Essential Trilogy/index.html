description

Steeped in romance and sentiment, a trilogy ring is one of the most romantic choices for an engagement ring because historically the three stones are symbolic of the past, the present and the future.

Selected for their superlative sparkle and fire, the round brilliant diamonds in our three stone round diamond trilogy engagement ring are secured in place by four-claw settings crafted with minimal metal to accentuate the scintillation of the stones.

A simple yet sophisticated design, our round diamond trilogy ring is available in different carat sizes, and in platinum, yellow, rose or white gold.
